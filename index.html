Who Are We?

Empower Mind Counselling and Therapy (EMCT) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization based in Malawi, dedicated to promoting mental health awareness and providing accessible counseling services. Founded in 2023, we focus on empowering young people and communities by breaking the stigma surrounding mental health and offering professional psychosocial support. Our mission is to foster resilience, promote well-being, and create a safe space for dialogue, healing, and growth.

Our Services

Youth Mental Health Counseling

We provide one-on-one counseling and therapy for young people, helping them cope with issues such as stress, trauma, and emotional well-being.

Community Outreach Programs

We conduct mental health awareness campaigns and educational workshops to break the stigma surrounding mental health in communities.

Professional Training & Workshops

We offer training and workshops to professionals and community leaders, building capacity in mental health support and advocacy.

our current Service areas

University of Malawi

Empower Mind Counselling and Therapy (EMCT) is present at the University of Malawi, providing mental health services, awareness programs, and support for students and staff.

Lilongwe University of Agriculture and Natural Resources

EMCT offers counseling and support services to the students of Lilongwe University, focusing on promoting mental health and wellbeing within the academic community.

Malawi University of Science and Applied Sciences

At Malawi University of Science and Applied Sciences, EMCT provides mental health education and therapeutic support to help students navigate academic pressures and personal challenges.

Domasi College of Education

EMCT’s chapter at Domasi College of Education focuses on fostering mental wellness among future educators, equipping them with the tools to support mental health in their communities.
